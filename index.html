ℹ️ Methodology
Kaplan-Meier (KM) Estimator — Non-parametric method per NIST/SEMATECH e-Handbook of Statistical Methods.
R(tᵢ) = R(tᵢ₋₁) × (nᵢ - dᵢ) / nᵢ  |  where nᵢ = units at risk, dᵢ = failures at time tᵢ
Censored observations (currently running wells) are included at their current run life and removed after their censoring time.
SE = R(t) × √[Σ dᵢ/(nᵢ(nᵢ-dᵢ))]  (Greenwood's formula)
MTTF = area under survival curve (trapezoidal integration)
